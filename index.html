How to play
  1. A famous artifact has been stolen. You are the Bureau's top field agent.
  2. In each city, interview three witnesses. Some point to the next destination; some describe the thief.
  3. Clues turn on geography, history, and — in Sacred Relics mode — Catholic tradition.
  4. Study the five suspect files. As you gather traits (hair, hobby, vehicle, feature), issue an arrest warrant.
  5. Catch the thief in the final city before time runs out, with the correct warrant in hand. Longer cases have more time — budget scales with the trail.
